# Evaluation Methodology  
## Well Made Decisions Lab

## Evaluation Goal

Measure structured reasoning quality, not conversational fluency.

---

## Rubric (0–2 per category)

1. Structural completeness
2. Objective clarity
3. Stakeholder rigor
4. Risk depth
5. Option diversity
6. Reversibility clarity
7. Devil’s advocate strength
8. Calibration of uncertainty
9. Citation quality
10. Actionability of next test

Maximum score: 20

---

## Test Set

15 standardized decision scenarios across:

- Career transitions
- Hiring decisions
- Market expansion
- Capital allocation
- Product launch
- Organizational design
- Personal leadership decisions
- Rebranding decision
- Acquisition investment decision
- New product development investment

---

## Pilot Observations

- Structured outputs are consistent.
- Retrieval works reliably with chapter segmentation.
- Citation density depends on clarity of user input.
