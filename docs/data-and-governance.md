# Data & Governance  
## Well Made Decisions Lab

## Governance Philosophy

Well Made Decisions Lab is designed to:

- Structure decisions responsibly
- Reduce cognitive bias
- Encourage calibrated uncertainty
- Avoid overconfidence
- Help business decision makers make and implement better decisions faster

---

## Data Collected (Pilot Version)

- First Name (optional)
- Email address
- Submission timestamp
- Report link (if generated)

Decision scenario text is not intentionally stored long-term. Roadmap might include saving aggregate, non-personalized data about the decision scenarios to inform future research and writing.

---

## Data Not Collected

- Payment data
- Legal case information
- Medical information
- Confidential corporate materials
- Sensitive personal identifiers

Users are warned not to submit confidential data.

---

## Data Retention

Pilot:
- Email data stored in Google Sheets.
- No persistent storage of decision content.

Future version:
- Supabase storage
- Defined retention windows
- Role-based access
- Secure environment variable handling

---

## Model Safety

The Lab does NOT:

- Provide legal advice
- Provide medical advice
- Provide financial investment advice
- Guarantee outcomes

It provides structured reasoning support only.

---

## Ethical AI Principles

- Structure before opinion
- Multiple-option analysis
- Reversibility bias
- Explicit uncertainty
- Citation discipline
